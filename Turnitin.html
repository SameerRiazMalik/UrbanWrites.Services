About Turnitin. It’ll blow your mind.

Turnitin is a plagiarism detection and originality checking tool that compares student writing to a database of other sources. It can help identify plagiarism, improve academic writing, and guide students on academic integrity. Turnitin uses text-matching, writing-style analysis, and metadata analysis to generate a Similarity Report that highlights matching content. Instructors can use this report to determine what passages are legitimate uses of existing language and what might be considered plagiarism.
